1. Sleep Well

Getting 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night boosts your immune system and keeps your mind sharp. Turn off electronics an hour before bedtime and keep your room dark and cool.

2. Drink Water

Stay hydrated by drinking at least 8 cups of water daily. Start your day with a glass of water to activate your metabolism.

3. Eat a Balanced Diet

Incorporate a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains into your meals. Avoid sugary drinks and processed foods for optimal health.

5. Manage Stress

Practice mindfulness, meditation, or deep breathing exercises to reduce stress levels. Even a 10-minute walk in nature can help clear your mind.
